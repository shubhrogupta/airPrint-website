About the Project

Since 15 decades the human race has relied on fossil fuels and till present we are directly or indirectly always using fossil fuels. These fuels have come a long way leading to urbanization and development of human race but at a price. Pollution. Soot being a major component. These small particles of soot add up and lead to deaths in big numbers. Premature deaths, asthma, and several other diseases are caused by high levels of pollution.

The Product

A revolutionary device which makes use of your particulate-matter-emitting vehicle by grabbing the black carbon by itâ€™s high tech filter and using that black carbon to make high quality ink.

The device is attached to the tailpipe of the vehicle. The exhaust will pass through the device and the carbon emmission is collected and turned into printer ink. The device filter works with HEPA technology and Ultra Compressible Graphene Aerogel Technology.

The filter contains graphene which increases the absorption capacity of the ink generator to give you the ink you deserve, the ink you desire and dream.

The device comes with an ultrasonic sensor along with pollution trackers. This helps drivers track nearby traffic and drive optimally. AI companies can also make use of this data which will allow the AI systems to monitor the traffic and the rush hours and help the government improve road connectivity.

Variants

We provide two variants of airPrint:


  • Soot filter: The basic filter which comes with the base model. It will simply take CO2 and convert it to ink.

  • Soot filter + Microfilter: This will also filter out oxides of NOx and SO2. It is not included with the base model.
